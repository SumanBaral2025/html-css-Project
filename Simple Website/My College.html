Bengal Institute of Science & Technology is a professionally managed academic institution affiliated to Sidho-Kanho-Birsha University, Purulia and Recognized & Approved By AICTE to impart management & technical education in Purulia, established in the year 2001. The Institute is managed by a group of academicians, professional mangers and social workers. As an academic institution, Bengal Institute of Science & Technology is intended to convey professional and technical education in the best possible manager which is the demand of new century. The Institute provide a unique combination of academic skill with real life exposure to impart professional knowledge to the young aspirants.
